ALETHEIA FABRICATION PROTOCOL

Material: InAs/Al Heterostructure (In-Situ MBE)
Process: E-Beam Lithography

Critical Steps

Mesa Etch: Wet etch (Citric/H2O2) to define the 3um wire.

Probe Write: Write the Middle Probe (Layer 30) using 100pA beam current.

Safety: DO NOT SONICATE. Nanowires are fragile.
